Our Operational Excellence

Detailed insight into our processes at DealCraft

DealCraft

DealCraft Partners Nigeria Ltd is an integrated commodity management company focused on bridging gaps in Nigeria's commodities ecosystem

Its operations span the entire agricultural commodity value chain, emphasizing sustainable practices, financial inclusion, and alignment with UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) such as No Poverty (SDG 1), Zero Hunger (SDG 2), Decent Work and Economic Growth (SDG 8), and Responsible Consumption and Production (SDG 12). The company provides end-to-end solutions to enhance farmer productivity, market access, and risk management, while promoting economic growth and community support.

Key Operational Areas

1

Farmer Productivity Enhancement

DealCraft supports farmers by providing access to essential inputs, agronomic training, climate-smart farming practices, and mechanization tools. This aims to improve crop yields, increase incomes, and boost agricultural productivity, directly contributing to food security and poverty reduction.

2

Aggregation & Storage

The company operates rural aggregation centers with digital inventory tracking and storage infrastructure. These services help minimize post-harvest losses, ensure efficient supply chain management, and maintain product quality through secure warehousing.

3

Financial Inclusion Initiatives

DealCraft links farmers and traders to credit, insurance, mobile payments, and input financing. By leveraging electronic warehouse receipt systems, it improves access to finance, enhances financial stability, and mitigates risks for stakeholders.

4

Trading Activities

Involved in sourcing commodities directly from farmers, ensuring off-take agreements, price transparency, and broader market access. This facilitates efficient trading, better price discovery, and overall economic growth in the commodities sector.

5

Export Operations

DealCraft manages compliance-driven export supply chains, including quality control, logistics partnerships, and international standards adherence. This enables seamless export of Nigerian commodities, expanding market opportunities for local producers.

6

Collateral Management Services

A core focus area, offering comprehensive solutions like stock monitoring, warehouse management, compliance audits, and risk assessments.

Collateral Management Phases

Pre-Engagement

Background checks on asset authenticity, warehouse evaluations for integrity and security, risk identification, process reviews, and goal alignment.

Engagement

Assignment of trained managers, implementation of advanced inventory systems, real-time reporting, corrective measures for risks, and surveillance for asset protection.

Post-Engagement

Closing audits, documentation handover, performance feedback, and strategic recommendations for future improvements.

DealCraft's operations are underpinned by a team with extensive experience in commodity trading, finance, and sustainability. Key strengths include a robust network of suppliers and partners, a commitment to integrity and quality, and the use of technology for monitoring and transparency. The company is headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria, and operates with a focus on long-term sustainability and client-centric excellence.
